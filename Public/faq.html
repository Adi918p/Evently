Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an event?

Create an account, select an event, choose the number of tickets, and confirm your booking.

Where can I see my bookings?

All your bookings are available in the "My Bookings" section of your profile.

Can I create my own event?

Users with Organizer privileges can create and manage events through the dashboard.

How do I contact support?

Visit the Support page and submit your query through the contact form.
